Conway's Game of Life

How life works

Each cell in the grid is either "alive" (green) or "dead" (white). Every turn of the game, the status of each cell will update depending on the cells around it.

Here are the rules:

  • For "alive" cells:
    • – If the cell has 2 or 3 neighbours, it stays alive
    • – If the cell has fewer than 2 or more than 3 neighbours, it dies

  • For "dead" cells:
    • – If the cell has exactly 3 neighbours, it comes to life
    • – If the cell has fewer than or more than 3 neighbours, it remains dead
